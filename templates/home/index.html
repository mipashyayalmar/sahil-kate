Steps to Get Started

In the evolving digital landscape, virtual business cards are now a vital tool for effective networking. These cards offer convenience by being easily accessible and eliminate the clutter of traditional paper cards. Digital cards enhance your professionalism during online meetings through platforms such as Zoom and Teams, allowing seamless contact sharing.

Quick Account Setup

Register your Equicard account effortlessly by providing some basic information. Get started with minimal input and create your digital business identity in no time.

Personalized Card Creation

Unleash your creativity by designing a personalized business card. Choose your color schemes, embed engaging videos, add web URLs, and link your social profiles to leave a lasting impression on your clients.

Instant Card Sharing

Make your presence known by sharing your digital business card anytime, anywhere. With just a tap, you can distribute your card to potential clients or partners, helping you build valuable connections.

QR Scanning

Additionally, offer your contacts the option to scan your QR code to quickly find and connect with your profile, ensuring seamless networking both online and in person.

Creative Demos of Professional Visiting Cards

Creative demos of professional visiting cards showcase innovative designs that combine aesthetics with functionality. These demos highlight unique elements like typography, colors, and materials to make memorable, impactful business cards.
